Anne Steuernagel

Doctoral Candidate · ifo Institute · FAU Erlangen-Nuremberg

I am a PhD candidate at the Ifo Institute and the FAU Erlangen-Nuremberg. This year, I am visiting Harvard University (Spring 2026) and the University of Calgary (Fall 2026). My research interests lie at the intersection of Labor, Public, and Health Economics with a focus on the economics of aging and labor market situation of older individuals. Specifically,... My single-author paper examines how job strain towards the end of working life shapes health post-retirement.
