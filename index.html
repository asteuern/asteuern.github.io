Anne Steuernagel

ifo Institute · FAU Erlangen-Nuremberg

I am a PhD candidate at the ifo Institute and FAU Erlangen-Nuremberg. This year, I am visiting Harvard University (Spring 2026) and the University of Calgary (Fall 2026).
My research interests lie at the intersection of labor, public, and health economics with a focus on the economics of aging and the labor market situation of older individuals.
My job-market paper examines how job strain towards the end of working life shapes health post-retirement.
